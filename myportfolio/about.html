Hi, I'm Christian Virrey.
Digital artist based in the Philippines.


I am passionate about my artworks. When I was a kid I already love drawing, I don't want my talent to go to waste, so it became my source of motivation. This passion led me to challenge myself to learn something new everyday.

I am still honing my skills. I was a traditional artist, the world is changing because of technology perhaps I taught myself how to do new media art. I also taught myself how to use Photoshop and stil learning to use Illustrator to improve the quality of my artworks.


Currently, I can work on digital drawing/paintings, photo editing/manipulations and also logo making.
